COGITO (Latin for "to plan" is a web tool that lets it's users plan out product/event marketing campaigns in great detail after taking into consideration a wide array of input parameters

For example, if a user wanted to plan his campaign for a science fest being hosted by his university, he would simply have to tell COGITO the following things

  • Target Audience
  • Location
  • Campaign Budget
  • Campaign team details: Restrictions, Preferences etc
  • Optimization Parameters: Travel expense, distance, proximity to university etc
  • Campaign Duration

COGITO analyses these constraints and solves the campaigning problem to arrive at an optimal solution

Cogito does the following things for the user(in the specified example):

  • Collects a database of all schools in Bangalore along with details like detailed address, email address, phone number, principal’s name, student population strength, etc..

  • Plans out daily campaign agendae for the various campaigning teams by considering various geographical and non – geographical parameters.

  • Calculates estimated travel expenses and suggests budget for campaign materials.

  • Updates the campaigners on a daily basis.

  • Automatically updates the database regarding responses at various schools.

  • Estimates probable event turnout as every day passes.

  • Updates the managerial team about every day’s work and assigns productivity ranks to the various campaigning teams.

  • And many more niche optimizations that can only be discussed in detail in future documentation.
